Find quick answers to common questions about our coconut supply, export processes, pricing, and logistics.

We supply a variety of premium coconuts including Pollachi Coconuts, Semi Husked Coconuts, Brown Fresh Coconuts, and Peravurani Coconuts. We cater to both domestic requirements and international export markets with carefully graded products.

Our minimum order quantity varies depending on the destination (domestic vs. international) and the specific product type. Typically, international export orders are fulfilled in standard 20ft or 40ft shipping containers. Please contact our sales team with your exact requirements for a customized quote.
Quality is our top priority. We source directly from trusted farms in Tamil Nadu. Every coconut undergoes careful manual inspection and is graded based on size, maturity, husk thickness, and water volume according to ISO 22000 standards before being packed.
For international export orders, we typically require an Irrevocable Letter of Credit (L/C) at sight or a Telegraphic Transfer (T/T) with a 40% advance deposit and the balance payable against scanned shipping documents. Domestic terms may vary and can be discussed directly with our team.
Domestic deliveries across India are usually dispatched within 2–5 working days of confirmed payment. Export shipment timelines depend on customs clearance, phytosanitary checks, and vessel schedules, but we pride ourselves on rapid and reliable logistics.
