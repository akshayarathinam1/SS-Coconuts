About SS Coconuts

Our Heritage of Excellence

Founded in 1989 in the heart of Tamil Nadu, SS Coconuts began as a small family-run farm with a commitment to delivering the finest coconuts. Over three decades, we have grown into one of India's most reliable coconut suppliers, serving clients across 50+ districts and exporting to 10+ countries.

After harnessing the best growing conditions Tamil Nadu has to offer, our coconuts are carefully harvested, graded, and dispatched with the highest quality standards — from farm to your doorstep.
